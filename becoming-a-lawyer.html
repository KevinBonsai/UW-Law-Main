Becoming a Lawyer

Why Study at UW Law?

Discover what being a lawyer can mean to you, enjoy experience-based opportunities, pursue public policy development through legislation, regulations, and lawsuits and embark on becoming a Leader for the Global Common Good.

Where To Begin?

  • Review your values, interests, talents, and personal characteristics and the skills lawyers need

  • Explore practice areas for lawyers and which ones might suit you

  • Seek help from your assigned attorney mentor and our accomplished faculty

  • Design your education around the knowledge, skills and real lawyering experience you will need.

Find Your Path

Prepare yourself for your future by choosing courses with a mix of knowledge, skills, and real-life lawyering practice.

  • Knowledge: Build Your Foundation
  • Skills: Develop Your Professional Tools
  • Lawyering: Explore Real Life Practice

Take It Into the World!

Your UW Law degree prepares you to be a lawyer who:

  • Resolves Disputes
  • Advises Clients
  • Facilitates Transactions
